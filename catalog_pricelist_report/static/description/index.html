Catalog Report

Catalog Report used to design catalog with image, barcode and stock multiple display options. To create a beautiful catalog open 'Product variants', then select the variants of the product you want to print. Under the 'Print' button located in the top middle of the screen, you can select the 'Catalog Pricelist Report' to generate the product catalog.

How to use

  • Generate the catalog under Product Variants by choosing products clicking on 'Print' button select Catalog Report.

Design catalog with product images and attributes.

  • Customize header Image by choosing the print header and uploading from a wizard.

  • Display product variant attributes sorted by attribute name.

Print product Barcodes

  • Integrate product barcode in your product catalog.

  • Select a barcode type:

    • Codabar
    • Code11
    • Code128
    • EAN13
    • EAN8
    • Extended39
    • Extended93
    • FIM
    • i2of5
    • MSI
    • Postnet
    • QR
    • Standard39
    • Standard93
    • USPS_4Stater

Product Pricelist

  • Display product prices per unit as per pricelist select.

  • Display product prices as per quantity without pricelist.

Product Stock

  • Display available and incoming stock per product.
